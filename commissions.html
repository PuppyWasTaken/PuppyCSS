The Process

Reach out

Message me on Discord with your ideas, references, and preferred commission type. I'll confirm availability.

Deposit & Mock Up

A $10 USD deposit is necessary following a mockup of your profile design. I'll send a preliminary mock up based on the information you provide which we can change and tweak together before moving to coding.

Coding

Once the mockup design is approved by you and finalized, I will begin coding it. You'll receive a link to the account where the profile is being coded so you can monitor progress updates and request small changes/adjustments. Please note: large adjustments and deviations from the approved mock up may incur extra costs.

Delivery

Once the remaining balance is settled, you'll receive a document with your code along with instructions for how to apply it to your account. I will make sure it is functioning properly on your end and make any fixes as necessary.

Terms

  • Deposits are non-refundable once mockup and coding has begun.
  • I retain the right to post finished commissions in my gallery unless private work is agreed on in advance.
  • You may not resell, redistribute, or claim commissioned profiles as your own.
  • Heavy scope changes after the finalized mockup stage may incur an additional fee.
  • I reserve the right to decline any commission concept.
  • By purchasing and receiving a commission from me, you agree that you will not redistribute the entire code or portions of the commissioned code. I do not consent to my commissions being redistributed for profit and not for profit.
